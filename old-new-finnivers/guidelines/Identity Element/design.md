Building blocks consist of basic shapes square, circle, triangle and quarter circle. This is a physical, graphic and illustrative universe that can grow, evolve, be played with, and inspire. ## Usage

These are mostly found in printed material, but can also be used in empty screens, error pages, or emails. These elements demand visual attention so be careful not to use them in situations where they could distract the user and take focus away from important tasks they need to perform.
